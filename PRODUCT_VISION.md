# Product Vision

## Mission

Help ordinary people make better investment decisions.

We do NOT predict markets.  
We do NOT promise returns.  
We help users make better decisions.

## Core Principle

Every screen must answer one question:

**What is the best financial decision for me today?**

## Product Philosophy

AI never makes the decision.

The Decision Engine is deterministic.

AI explains.

Everything should be explainable.

## Daily Brief

## Long-Term Vision

Become the operating system for personal investing.

Not another stock tracker.

Not another chatbot.

A trusted decision companion.

## Rule

Every feature must answer:

**Does this help the user make a better investment decision?**

If not, it should not be built.
